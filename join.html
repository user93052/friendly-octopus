    How to Join

    Source: Jason. P. on Creative Commons

    Press "sign up" below to fill out the form! Once you've done so:

    • You'll be added to our mailing list for meeting reminders and event updates
    • You'll be entered into random giveaways for free Chinese calligraphy supplies
    • You'll receive updates on news relating to Chinese calligraphy in the Cornell community, along with the rest of the art world
    • There's no pressure or commitment—come to as many or as few meetings as you like!

    Membership Details

    Source: UI International Programs on Creative Commons
    Who can join
    All Cornell students, regardless of experience
    Dues
    None—membership is completely free
    Materials
    Provided during meetings (brushes, ink, paper, etc.)
    Meeting times
    Every Sunday, 4:30 p.m. – 6:00 p.m., at Rockfeller Hall in room 343
    Communication
    We use email for announcements and event coordination

    If you'd like to get involved more deeply, we encourage you to apply for the leadership team (see Team for application information).

    Not sure you want to join yet?

    Feel free to drop by our next meeting this Sunday, 4:30 p.m. – 6:00 p.m., in Rockefeller Hall, room 343, to check out what we do and meet the team—no pressure, just come say hi!

    And remember, signing up is completely non-committal! Feel free to come to as many or as few meetings as you like, but it's a great way to make progress and stay motivated.
